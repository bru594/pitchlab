How it works
From search to sent in minutes

A simple four-step workflow that gets you from zero to a personalized pitch without the manual research.

01

Find Leads

Search by location and business type. Get a list of real local businesses pulled from Google Maps.

02

Audit Their Site

One click scores their website on speed, mobile, SEO, and design. Shows you exactly what's wrong.

03

Generate Pitch

AI writes a cold email, call script, and SMS based on their specific audit results. Specific, not generic.

04

Send & Follow Up

Send outreach directly from PitchLab. Track opens and replies. Auto-stop sequences on reply.
